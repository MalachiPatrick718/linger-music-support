Linger-Music Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [Today’s Date]

Linger-Music (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Location Data: Used to show music drops near you. We do not store your exact location permanently.
  • Music Data: We collect the song you drop and your chosen emoji avatar. No personal listening history is stored.
  • Account Data: If you connect Spotify, we only use your account to fetch the currently playing song. We do not access or store your Spotify credentials.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core app features (music drops, discovery, notifications)
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To send notifications about music drops and likes

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only used to provide app functionality.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

Children’s Privacy

Linger-Music is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@lingermusic.app.
